Jigstack DAO – Decentralized Autonomous Organization for token launch, staking, NFT & wallet platform

Jigstack DAO has partnered with us to develop a comprehensive decentralized finance (DeFi) solution, including branding, application design, and a non-custodial wallet. Our collaboration began at the design phase and continued through the full product life cycle to ensure the best possible user experience. To achieve this goal, we focused on developing a user-friendly interface and pixel-perfect design. By working closely with Jigstack DAO throughout the project, we were able to deliver a high-quality, fully-fledged DeFi solution that meets the needs of our users.

In order to showcase all of Jigstack's eight products on the home page, we implemented a scrolling feature in the hero section. As the user scrolls down the page, the highlighted product will change, allowing for an effective and visually appealing way to present all of the products to the user. This solution allows for an efficient use of space on the home page while still providing visibility for all of Jigstack's products.

Responsive design ensures that a website is easy to use and provides an optimal viewing experience on all devices, from desktop computers to smartphones. It improves user experience and accessibility, and can potentially lead to higher conversion rates. It is essential for modern web development.

Stackbank

As a crypto staking platform, it was important to design a user interface that was intuitive and easy to use for our target audience. We began by conducting user research to understand the needs and goals of our users, as well as any potential pain points they might experience while using our platform.

Based on this research, we created wireframes and prototypes to test and iterate on different design solutions. We also made sure to include clear calls to action and prominent buttons to allow users to easily initiate staking actions. Overall, our goal was to create a staking platform that was not only functional, but also visually appealing and enjoyable to use. By conducting user research and testing various design solutions, we were able to deliver a high-quality staking platform that met the needs of our users.

Jwallet

The Jigstack Wallet has integrated DEX functionality allowing users to gain access to over 1900 coin pairs and over 1.7b worth of liquidity to swap with.

To design the DEX feature, the Jigstack team conducted extensive user research to understand the needs and preferences of their target audience. They learned that users valued simplicity and ease of use, as well as strong security measures to protect their assets.

With these insights in mind, the team designed a streamlined interface that made it easy for users to navigate and trade on the DEX. They also implemented robust security measures, such as two-factor authentication and cold storage, to ensure that users' assets were safe.

Responsive matters

Having a wallet that is designed to be responsive ensures that the user experience is consistent and seamless, regardless of the device being used. It also makes it easier for users to manage their cryptocurrency holdings on the go, which can be especially important in the fast-paced world of cryptocurrency trading.
